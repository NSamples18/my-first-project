Adoption Form

Thank you for choosing adoption—your compassion is about to change a life forever! At Safe Haven Dog Shelter, we work hard to ensure each adoption is a perfect match, not just for the dogs in our care, but for the families who welcome them home. This short form helps us understand your needs, lifestyle, and the kind of pup you're hoping to adopt. It also allows us to ensure the dog you’ve chosen will thrive in your care. Once submitted, our team will contact you within 2–3 business days to walk you through the next steps, schedule a meet-and-greet, and answer any questions you have about the adoption process.
